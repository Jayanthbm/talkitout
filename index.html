About Us

Talk it out was an idea born in July 2020 that primarily aimed at promoting mental health awareness.
Here at Talk It Out we promote the importance of mental health under the guidance of our psychologist.
Our team aims to build a community of people who understand the significance of mental health and it’s impact.
We do not offer any clinical services although we do have a psychologist on board.
We strongly believe that mental health should be given equal importance as physical health.
All we want you to do is, "TALK IT OUT.." And, Oh! Don’t forget to smile today

Meet The Team

Disha Begani


Psychologist

I have completed my Masters from Calcutta University in Clinical & Forensic Psychology. I am currently working as a Head program coordinator ( psychotherapy) at Good karma, where I am working under the supervision of Dr. Ranjan Ghosh and I am responsible for conducting in-person and online therapy for clients suffering from a vast range of mental and emotional disturbances. I have experience in the diagnosis of mental disorders, conducting various tests & assessments for over 50 clients working at some of the well known mental health institutions of Kolkata like Antara, Pavlov, INK, etc. Talk it out is a platform that is very close to my heart because before wanting the change to take place it is extremely important to work in the direction to bring that change and Talk it out gives that direction we are working on to bring the awareness that how important mental health is for our well being.

Riya Saini


Executive Director

I am an engineering graduate, currently working as a Junior Research Fellow at DA-IICT. I joined Snigdha to help create this platform where people can talk about their issues and ease their mind. The lockdown forced me to realise how important it is to go out and talk to people whom you can trust not to judge you. Through Talk it Out, I want to be able to reach out to as many people as possible and spread awareness about importance of maintaining their mental health.

Snigdha Mishra


Founder

I am an Engineering graduate from the city of Bengaluru. Currently working at an Ed tech Startup, Classplus. I've always been passionate about creating a social impact and bringing about a larger overall change. At the wake of the pandemic I felt an alarming need to promote mental health awareness. TALK IT OUT to me is a contribution to the society through which I want people to know the importance of mental health, to acknowledge their issues and speak about it.

Mission
